# Getting Started with KimuX

Welcome to KimuX! Here's your quick onboarding guide to get up and running in minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account
- Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
- Enter your email, password, and company details
- Verify your email address (check your inbox)

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile
- Add your company logo and branding colors
- Set your timezone and currency preferences
- Invite team members from Settings → Team

## Step 3: Connect Your Data Sources
- Go to **Settings → Integrations**
- Connect your ad accounts (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- Link your CRM or import contacts via CSV
- Optional: Connect Shopify, Stripe, or other tools

## Step 4: Create Your First Campaign
- Navigate to **Campaigns** in the main menu
- Click **New Campaign** and select your objective
- Set your target audience and budget
- Review and launch!

## Step 5: Set Up Your Dashboard
- Customize widgets to show metrics that matter to you
- Set up email alerts for important events
- Explore the AI Dashboard for automated insights

## Step 6: Explore Key Features
- **CRM**: Manage leads and contacts in one place
- **Affiliate Hub**: Build your affiliate program
- **AI Dashboard**: Get predictive analytics and recommendations
- **Reports**: Generate detailed performance reports
